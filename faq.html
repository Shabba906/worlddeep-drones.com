Common Questions About Our Drone Services

What is your service area?

We primarily serve [johannesburg south]. Please contact us to inquire about events outside this region.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for popular dates, to ensure availability. Ideally, 3-6 months in advance for weddings.

What happens if there is bad weather?

Safety is our top priority. In case of unsuitable weather conditions (heavy rain, strong winds), we will work with you to reschedule the drone service.

What kind of output will I receive?

You will receive high-resolution digital photos and/or edited video footage, depending on your chosen package.
